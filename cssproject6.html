South Point School and college

In 2003 South Point School and College started its glorious journey with a profound interest of Founding Chairman M. A. Rashid to meet the needs of grooming and moulding the students to be part of a forward-looking, progressive global society. The immaculate leadership of former principal of Viqarunnesa Noon School and College Mrs. Hamida Ali has been an ever inspiration for South Point School and College family. The college has already been able to accumulate astounding recognition and goodwill under the dynamic leadership of Founder Principal Mrs. Hamida Ali.

Following its outstanding success, South Point College Baridhara Campus is commencing Academic function in English Version for level eleven and twelve (HSC) for 2019-20 session. A veteran faculty with their high level expertise are ready to transform pupils into a new height in shaping this premier school with optimal teaching and learning attainment. We, at South Point School and College (SPSC), believe that Schools are living spirits that influence lives of students.

South Point School & College offers a complete primary, elementary, and secondary school program leading to the PEC, JSC, SSC and HSC examinations taken by the Bangladesh Government.[15][16] Only the Malibagh branch, Mirpur branch and Banani branch has English-medium and only the Banani branch is an only English-medium branch. The rest of the branches either are English version, Bangla-medium or Bangla version.

South Point School and College does not have any official team in any sports but sports like cricket, football, and badminton are widely practiced across branches. An official inter-school cricket tournament was supposed to take place in Baridhara campus's ground on March 17, 2020, due to Bangabandhu's 100th birthday celebration but was later cancelled due to schools closing in Bangladesh from March 17 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
